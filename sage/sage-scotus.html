SVD and the Supreme Court

Evaluate the first cell below will load a 9x9 matrix A that shows the percentage of times that the 9 justices on the Supreme Court during the 2nd Rehnquist era agreed with one another. There are also some additional functions:

  • outer(u,v), which computes the outer product of vectors u and v.
  • plot_sv(A) shows a plot of the singular values of A.
  • display_matrix(A) shows a visual representation of the matrix. Red is -1, black is 0, and white is 1 with intermediate values a combination of those. For instance, light gray is close to 1 while dark red is negative and close to 0.
  • A is defined so that U, S, V = A.SVD() works well.
  • Also, A.singular_values() gives the singular values of A.
