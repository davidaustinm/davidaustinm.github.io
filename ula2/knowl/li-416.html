\begin{equation*} \Sigma = \begin{bmatrix} \sigma_1 \amp 0 \amp 0 \\ 0 \amp \sigma_2 \amp 0 \end{bmatrix} \end{equation*}

and verify that \(A = U\Sigma V^T\text{.}\)
