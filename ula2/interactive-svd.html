The four sliders at the top of this figure enable us to choose a \(2\times2\) matrix \(A\text{.}\) Below on the left, we see the unit circle and the red unit vector \(\xvec\text{.}\) Notice that we can vary \(\xvec\) by clicking in the head of the vector and dragging it to a new unit vector.
