\(A\) has 5 rows.

---


\(A\) has 5 columns.

---

Every row of \(A\) has a pivot position.
 #paired
---

There is a vector \(\bvec\) for which the equation \(A\xvec = \bvec\) is inconsistent.

---

The equation \(A\xvec = \bvec\) is consistent for every vector \(\bvec\text{.}\)

 #paired

---

The columns of \(A\) span \(\real^5\text{.}\)
