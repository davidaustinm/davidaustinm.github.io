Answer 3.4.5.10.1.

  1. Column replacements do not change the determinant because \(\det R=1\text{.}\)

  2. Scaling a column by \(r\) multiplies the determinant by \(r\) because \(\det S=r\text{.}\)

  3. Interchanges change the sign of the determinant because \(\det P=-1\text{.}\)

  4. \(\det A = -12\text{.}\)
