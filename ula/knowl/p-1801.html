We begin in an initial state \(\xvec_0= \ctwovec{0}{1000}\text{.}\) The state one day later will be the vector \(\xvec_1 = T(\xvec_0) = A\xvec_0\text{.}\) In the example from our activity, we have

\begin{equation*} A = \left[\begin{array}{rr} 0.7 \amp 0.5 \\ 0.3 \amp 0.5 \\ \end{array}\right]\text{.} \end{equation*}

Therefore,

\begin{equation*} \xvec_1 = A\xvec_0 = \left[\begin{array}{rr} 0.7 \amp 0.5 \\ 0.3 \amp 0.5 \\ \end{array}\right] \ctwovec{0}{1000} =\ctwovec{500}{500}\text{.} \end{equation*}
