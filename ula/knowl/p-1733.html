More generally, we will write

\begin{equation*} \evec_1 = \left[\begin{array}{r} 1 \\ 0 \\ \vdots \\ 0 \end{array}\right], \evec_2 = \left[\begin{array}{r} 0 \\ 1 \\ \vdots \\ 0 \end{array}\right], \ldots, \evec_n = \left[\begin{array}{r} 0 \\ 0 \\ \vdots \\ 1 \end{array}\right] \end{equation*}

so that

\begin{equation*} T(\evec_j) = \left[\begin{array}{rrrr} \vvec_1 \amp \vvec_2 \amp \ldots \amp \vvec_n \end{array}\right] \evec_j = \vvec_j\text{.} \end{equation*}
