1. We find \(\bvec_1\) and \(\bvec_2\) by solving the equations

    \begin{equation*} A\bvec_1=\twovec{1}{0}, A\bvec_2=\twovec{0}{1}\text{.} \end{equation*}

    This gives

    \begin{equation*} B = \left[ \begin{array}{rr} -1 \amp -2 \\ -2 \amp -3 \\ \end{array}\right]\text{.} \end{equation*}

  2. It is true, in this case, that \(AB=BA=I\text{.}\)

  3. We have seen that \(I\xvec = \xvec\text{.}\)

  4. If \(\bvec=\twovec{5}{-2}\text{,}\) then \(\xvec=B\bvec=\twovec{-1}{-4}\) is the solution to the equation \(A\xvec=\bvec\text{.}\)
