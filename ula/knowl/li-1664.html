Item 3.1.5.2.c.

We can do this by constructing matrices that define reflections, such as

\begin{equation*} \left[\begin{array}{rr} 1 \amp 0 \\ 0 \amp -1 \\ \end{array}\right], \left[\begin{array}{rr} -1 \amp 0 \\ 0 \amp 1 \\ \end{array}\right], \left[\begin{array}{rr} 0 \amp 1 \\ 1 \amp 0 \\ \end{array}\right]\text{.} \end{equation*}
