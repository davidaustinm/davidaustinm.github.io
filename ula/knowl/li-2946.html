Item 4.4.5.1.c.

This is a saddle because there are two real eigenvalues \(\lambda_1=1.2\) and \(\lambda_2=0.5\) with associated eigenvectors \(\vvec_1=\twovec{-2}1\) and \(\vvec_2=\twovec{-1}1\text{.}\) Most trajectories will move away from the origin along the line defined by the eigenvector \(\vvec_1\text{.}\)
