We form the augmented matrix and find a triangular matrix that is row equivalent:

\begin{equation*} \left[\begin{array}{rr|r} 3 \amp 1 \amp k \\ -1 \amp 1 \amp -2 \\ 1 \amp 2 \amp 5 \\ \end{array}\right] \sim \left[\begin{array}{rr|r} -1 \amp 1 \amp -2 \\ 0 \amp 1 \amp 1 \\ 0 \amp 0 \amp k-10 \\ \end{array}\right]\text{.} \end{equation*}

This represents a consistent system when \(k=10\text{.}\)
