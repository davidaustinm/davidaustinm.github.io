Item 4.1.4.8.c.

For the matrix \(A\text{,}\) \(\vvec_1=\threevec{1}{1}{1}\) with associated eigenvalue \(\lambda_1=3\text{,}\) and \(\vvec_2=\threevec{-1}{1}{0}\) and \(\vvec_3=\threevec{-1}{0}{1}\) with associated eigenvalue \(\lambda_2=\lambda_3 = 0\text{.}\)

For \(B\text{,}\) \(\vvec_1=\threevec{1}{2}{3}\) with associated eigenvalue \(\lambda_1=6\) and \(\vvec_2=\threevec{-1}{1}{0}\) and \(\vvec_3=\threevec{-1}{0}{1}\) with associated eigenvalue \(\lambda_2=\lambda_3=0\text{.}\)
