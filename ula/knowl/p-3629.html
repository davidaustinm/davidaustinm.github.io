Notice that an eigenvector \(\vvec\) must be in the column space \(\col(A)\) because \(A\vvec=\lambda\vvec\text{.}\) The column space of this matrix is spanned by \(\vvec_1=\threevec{1}{1}{1}\) and we notice that \(A\vvec_1 = 3\vvec_1\text{.}\) Therefore, \(\vvec_1\) is an eigenvector with associated eigenvalue \(\lambda_1=3\text{.}\)
