Item 4.1.4.5.b.

The corresponding matrix transformation stretches vectors horizontally by a factor of \(2\) and reflects them while stretching by a factor of \(4\) vertically. We have eigenvectors \(\vvec_1=\twovec{1}{0}\) with associated eigenvalue \(\lambda_1=-2\) and \(\vvec_2=\twovec{0}{1}\) with \(\lambda_2=4\text{.}\)
