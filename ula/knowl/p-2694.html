Consider now the vector \(\xvec=\threevec{3}{1}{-2}\text{.}\) If we would like to express \(\xvec\) in the coordinate system defined by \(\bcal\text{,}\) then we compute

\begin{equation*} \coords{\xvec}{\bcal} = P^{-1}_{\bcal}\xvec = \left[\begin{array}{rrr} \frac14 \amp \frac 12 \amp -\frac38 \\ -\frac14 \amp \frac12 \amp -\frac18 \\ \frac14 \amp \frac12 \amp \frac18 \\ \end{array}\right] \threevec{3}{1}{-2} = \threevec{2}{0}{1}\text{.} \end{equation*}
