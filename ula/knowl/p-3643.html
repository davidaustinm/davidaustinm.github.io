Vectors along the \(y\)-axis are unchanged so \(\threevec{0}{1}{0}\) is an eigenvector with associated eigenvalue \(\lambda=1\text{.}\) Vectors in the \(xz\)-plane are multiplied by \(-1\) so \(\threevec{1}{0}{0}\) and \(\threevec{0}{0}{1}\) are eigenvectors with associated eigenvalue \(\lambda=-1\text{.}\)
