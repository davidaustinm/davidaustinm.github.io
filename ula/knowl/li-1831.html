Item f.

If we define \(B\) to be the matrix whose columns are \(\twovec{1}{2}\) and \(\twovec{-2}{1}\text{,}\) then

\begin{equation*} \coords{\xvec}{\bcal} = A^{-1}\xvec = A^{-1}B\coords{\xvec}{\ccal}\text{.} \end{equation*}

Therefore, \(C=A^{-1}B = \left[\begin{array}{rr} -\frac{3}{7} \amp -\frac{4}{7} \\ \frac{8}{7} \amp -\frac{1}{7} \end{array}\right]\text{.}\)
