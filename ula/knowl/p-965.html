Given matrices \(A\) and \(B\text{,}\) we will form their product \(AB\) by first writing \(B\) in terms of its columns:

\begin{equation*} B = \left[\begin{array}{rrrr} \vvec_1 \amp \vvec_2 \amp \ldots \amp \vvec_p \end{array}\right]\text{.} \end{equation*}

We then define

\begin{equation*} AB = \left[\begin{array}{rrrr} A\vvec_1 \amp A\vvec_2 \amp \ldots \amp A\vvec_p \end{array}\right]\text{.} \end{equation*}
