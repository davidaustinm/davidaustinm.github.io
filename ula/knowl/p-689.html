We have

\begin{equation*} \left[\begin{array}{rrr|r} 1 \amp 2 \amp 3 \amp 1 \\ 4 \amp 5 \amp 6 \amp 4 \\ a \amp b \amp c \amp 9 \\ \end{array}\right] \sim \left[\begin{array}{rrr|r} 1 \amp 2 \amp 3 \amp 1 \\ 0 \amp 1 \amp 2 \amp 0 \\ 0 \amp 0 \amp a-2b+c \amp 9-a \\ \end{array}\right]\text{.} \end{equation*}

  1. If \(a - 2b + c = 0\) and \(9-a\neq 0\text{,}\) then the system will be inconsistent. We can choose \(a=b=c=0\text{.}\)

  2. We want there to be a pivot position in every column so we choose \(a-2b+c\neq 0\text{.}\) For instance \(a=1\text{,}\) \(b=0\text{,}\) and \(c=0\) works.

  3. We need for the third and fourth columns to lack pivot positions so we choose \(a+c-2b=0\) and \(a = 9\text{.}\) For instance, \(a=9\text{,}\) \(b=0\text{,}\) and \(c=-9\) works.
