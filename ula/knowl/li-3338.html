Item c.

We first multiply the first row by \(-10000\) and add to the second row. This gives

\begin{equation*} \begin{array}{l} \left[\begin{array}{rr|r} 0.0001 \amp 1 \amp 1 \\ 0 \amp -9999 \amp -9998 \\ \end{array}\right] \\ \approx \left[\begin{array}{rr|r} 0.0001 \amp 1 \amp 1 \\ 0 \amp -10000 \amp -10000 \\ \end{array}\right] \\ \sim \left[\begin{array}{rr|r} 0.0001 \amp 1 \amp 1 \\ 0 \amp 1 \amp 1 \\ \end{array}\right] \text{.} \end{array} \end{equation*}

This tells us that \(y\approx 1\text{.}\)
