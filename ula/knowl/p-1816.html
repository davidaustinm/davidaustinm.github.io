  1. Since \(A\) is a \(3\times3\) matrix, we have \(T:\real^3\to\real^3\text{.}\) Since \(B\) is a \(2\times3\) matrix, we have \(S:\real^3\to\real^2\text{.}\)

  2. \(T\left(\threevec{1}{-3}{2}\right) = A\twovec{1}{-3}{2} = \threevec{6}{-1}{-6}\text{.}\)

  3. \(S\left(\threevec{-2}{2}{1}\right) = A\twovec{-2}{2}{1}= \twovec{-6}{0}\text{.}\)

  4. \(S\circ T\left(\threevec{1}{-3}{2}\right) = BA\threevec{1}{-3}{2} = \twovec{7}{-1}\text{.}\)

  5. \(C = BA = \left[ \begin{array}{rrr} 2 \amp -3 \amp -2 \\ 5 \amp 6 \amp 6 \\ \end{array}\right] \text{.}\)
