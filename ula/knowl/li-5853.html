Item 7.5.7.4.d.

The fourth components, the ones that correspond to house prices, of \(\uvec_1\) and \(\uvec_2\) are \(-0.65\) and \(-0.03\) respectively. This shows us that \(\uvec_2\) doesn't contribute much to the house price. Moving in the negative \(\uvec_1\) direction causes the house price to go up so the more expensive homes are on the left.
