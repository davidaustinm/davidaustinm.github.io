The column space \(\col(A)\) contains all the vectors \(\bvec\) for which the equation \(A\xvec = \bvec\) is consistent. The null space \(\nul(A)\) describes the solution space to the equation \(A\xvec = \zerovec\text{,}\) and its dimension tells us whether this equation has a unique solution.
