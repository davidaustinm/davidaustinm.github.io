Exercise 6.

Suppose that \(A\) is a \(2\times2\) matrix having eigenvectors

\begin{equation*} \vvec_1=\twovec{2}{1}, \qquad \vvec_2=\twovec{-1}{2} \end{equation*}

and associated eigenvalues \(\lambda_1=2\) and \(\lambda_2=-3\text{.}\)

  1. If \(\xvec=\twovec{5}{0}\text{,}\) find the vector \(A^4\xvec\text{.}\)

  2. Find the vectors \(A\twovec10\) and \(A\twovec01\text{.}\)

  3. What is the matrix \(A\text{?}\)
