We can form the linear combinations:

  • When \(a = 2\) and \(b=0\text{,}\) the linear combination is \(\twovec{2}{4}\text{.}\)

  • When \(a = 1\) and \(b=1\text{,}\) the linear combination is \(\twovec{3}{3}\text{.}\)

  • When \(a = 0\) and \(b=-1\text{,}\) the linear combination is \(\twovec{-1}{-2}\text{.}\)
