We have the system corresponding to the augmented matrix

\begin{equation*} \left[\begin{array}{rrr|r} 2 \amp 0 \amp 4 \amp 10 \\ -1 \amp 3 \amp 4 \amp 1 \\ -2 \amp 1 \amp -2 \amp -8 \\ \end{array}\right] \sim \left[\begin{array}{rrr|r} 1 \amp 0 \amp 2 \amp 5 \\ 0 \amp 1 \amp 2 \amp 2 \\ 0 \amp 0 \amp 0 \amp 0 \\ \end{array}\right] \end{equation*}

from which we conclude that \(\bvec\) may be expressed as a linear combination of \(\vvec_1\text{,}\) \(\vvec_2\text{,}\) and \(\vvec_3\) provided that the weights are related by \(c_1=5-2c_3\) and \(c_2=2-2c_3\text{.}\)
