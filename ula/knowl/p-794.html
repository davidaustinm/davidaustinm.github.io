In fact, we may easily describe the associated linear system in terms of the vectors \(\vvec\text{,}\) \(\wvec\text{,}\) and \(\bvec\text{.}\) Notice that the augmented matrix we found in our example was \(\left[ \begin{array}{rr|r} 3 \amp 4 \amp -11 \\ -1 \amp 3 \amp -18 \end{array} \right].\) The first two columns of this matrix are \(\vvec\) and \(\wvec\) and the rightmost column is \(\bvec\text{.}\) As shorthand, we will write this augmented matrix replacing the columns with their vector representation:

\begin{equation*} \left[ \begin{array}{rr|r} \vvec \amp \wvec \amp \bvec \end{array} \right]\text{.} \end{equation*}
