We can form the matrix

\begin{equation*} C_{\bcal}=\left[\begin{array}{rr} \vvec_1 \amp \vvec_2 \end{array}\right] = \left[\begin{array}{rr} -2 \amp 2 \\ 2 \amp 1 \\ \end{array}\right] \end{equation*}

so that \(\xvec=C_{\bcal}\coords{\xvec}{\bcal}\text{.}\) We then need to solve the equation \(C_{\bcal}\coords{\xvec}{\bcal} = \twovec{60}{90}\text{.}\) This gives \(\coords{\xvec}{\bcal} = \twovec{20}{50}\text{.}\)
