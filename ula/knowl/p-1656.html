Since the columns of \(A\) are linearly independent, the only solution to the homogeneous equation \(A\yvec=\zerovec\) is \(\yvec=\zerovec\text{.}\) We know that \(A(B-C)\xvec = A[(B-C)\xvec]=\zerovec\) for all vectors \(\xvec\text{.}\) This means that \((B-C)\xvec=\zerovec\) or \(B\xvec=C\xvec\) for all vectors \(\xvec\text{.}\) This implies that \(B=C\text{.}\)
