  1. Find the singular values of \(A\text{.}\) What is \(\rank(A)\text{?}\)

  2. Find the sequence of matrices \(A_1\text{,}\) \(A_2\text{,}\) \(A_3\text{,}\) and \(A_4\) where \(A_k\) is the rank \(k\) approximation of \(A\text{.}\)
