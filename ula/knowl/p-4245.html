This activity demonstrates the type of questions we will be considering. In particular, we will assume that we have an initial vector \(\xvec_0\) and a matrix \(A\) and define \(\xvec_{k+1} = A\xvec_k\text{.}\) The eigenvalues and eigenvectors of \(A\) provide the key that helps us understand how the vectors \(\xvec_k\) evolve and enables us to make long-range predictions.
