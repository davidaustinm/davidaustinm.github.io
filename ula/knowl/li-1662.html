Item 3.1.5.2.a.

\(A=\left[\begin{array}{rr} \frac1{\sqrt{2}} \amp -\frac1{\sqrt{2}} \\ \frac1{\sqrt{2}} \amp \frac1{\sqrt{2}} \\ \end{array}\right]\text{.}\) To undo the rotation, we will perform a \(45^\circ\) clockwise rotation, which is defined by the matrix \(A^{-1}=\left[\begin{array}{rr} \frac1{\sqrt{2}} \amp \frac1{\sqrt{2}} \\ -\frac1{\sqrt{2}} \amp \frac1{\sqrt{2}} \\ \end{array}\right]\text{.}\)
