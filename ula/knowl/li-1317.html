Item 2.5.5.4.c.

The matrix that defines \(T\) is

\begin{equation*} A = \left[\begin{array}{rrr} \vvec_1 \amp \vvec_2 \amp \vvec_3 \\ \end{array}\right] = \left[\begin{array}{rrr} 2 \amp -1 \amp -1 \\ 1 \amp 1 \amp -2 \\ \end{array}\right] \sim \left[\begin{array}{rrr} 1 \amp 0 \amp -1 \\ 0 \amp 1 \amp -1 \\ \end{array}\right]\text{.} \end{equation*}

This shows that the solutions to \(T(\xvec) = A\xvec = \zerovec\) are \(\xvec=x_3\threevec{1}{1}{1}\text{.}\)
