Item 4.4.5.7.h.

For large values of \(n\text{,}\) we have \(\xvec_n\approx\frac1{\sqrt5}\lambda_1^n\twovec{\lambda_1}{1}\text{,}\) which says that \(F_{n+1} \approx \frac1{\sqrt5}\lambda_1^{n+1}\) and \(F_{n} \approx \frac1{\sqrt5}\lambda_1^{n}\text{.}\) This gives the ratio \(F_{n+1}/F_n\approx \lambda_1\text{.}\)
