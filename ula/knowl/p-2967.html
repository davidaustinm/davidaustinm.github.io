To convert from \(RGB\) to \(YC_bC_r\text{,}\) we multiply by \(P_{\bcal}^{-1}\) so that

  1. \(\displaystyle \threevec{Y}{C_b}{C_r} = \threevec{76.2}{-43.0}{127.5}\)

  2. \(\displaystyle \threevec{Y}{C_b}{C_r} = \threevec{29.1}{127.5}{-20.7}\)

  3. \(\displaystyle \threevec{Y}{C_b}{C_r} = \threevec{255}{0}{0}\)

  4. \(\displaystyle \threevec{Y}{C_b}{C_r} = \threevec{0}{0}{0}\)
