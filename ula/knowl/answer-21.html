Answer 1.3.5.6.1.

  1. The exact solution is

    \begin{equation*} x=9, y=-36, z=30 \end{equation*}

    while the approximate solution is

    \begin{equation*} x=8.9, y=-35, z=29\text{.} \end{equation*}

  2. This solution to the first system is \(x=2\) and \(y=0\text{.}\) However, with just a small change in the system, we find the solution \(x=1\) and \(y=1\text{.}\)
