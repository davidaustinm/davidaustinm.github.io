Solution 4.3.5.10.1.

  1. Vectors are expanded horizontally and compressed vertically.

  2. Vectors are compressed both horizontally and vertically.

  3. Vectors are expanded both horizontally and vertically.

  4. There are eigenvectors \(\vvec_1=\twovec11\) with \(\lambda_1 = 1.2\) and \(\vvec_2=\twovec{-1}1\) with \(\lambda_2=0.7\text{.}\) Vectors are expanded in the direction of \(\vvec_1\) and compressed in the direction of \(\vvec_2\text{.}\)

  5. Because both eigenvalues have an absolute value smaller than \(1\text{,}\) vectors will be compressed in the direction of both eigenvectors and hence be pulled into the origin.
