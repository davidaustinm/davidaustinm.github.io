The corresponding system of equations is

\begin{equation*} \begin{alignedat}{3} x \amp \amp \amp {}={} \amp 3 \\ \amp \amp y \amp {}={} \amp 0 \\ \amp \amp 0 \amp {}={} \amp 0 \\ \end{alignedat} \end{equation*}

The third equation is satisfied for any values of \(x\) and \(y\text{.}\) Therefore, we see that the only solution is \((x,y) = (3,0)\text{.}\)
