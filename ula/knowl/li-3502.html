Item 5.2.4.2.a.

The power method tells us that the dominant eigenvalue is \(\lambda_1=-6\) with associated eigenvector \(\vvec_1=\twovec{0.5}{1}\text{.}\) The inverse power method tells us that the eigenvalue having the smallest absolute value is \(\lambda_2=2\) with associated eigenvector \(\vvec_2=\twovec{-0.5}1\text{.}\)
