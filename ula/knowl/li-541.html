Item 2.1.4.4.c.

We ask to write the vector \(\threevec{342}{385}{3.4}\) as a linear combination of the two cereal vectors. This leads to the augmented matrix

\begin{equation*} \left[\begin{array}{rr|r} 111 \amp 120 \amp 342 \\ 140 \amp 105 \amp 385 \\ 1.2 \amp 1.0 \amp 3.4 \\ \end{array}\right] \sim \left[\begin{array}{rr|r} 1 \amp 0 \amp 2 \\ 0 \amp 1 \amp 1 \\ 0 \amp 0 \amp 0 \\ \end{array}\right]\text{.} \end{equation*}

This means that you had two servings of Frosted Flakes and one serving of Cocoa Puffs.
