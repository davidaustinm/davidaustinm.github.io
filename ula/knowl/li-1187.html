If 1000 trucks begin at \(P\text{,}\) that evening we find that 70% of them are at \(P\) with the remaining 30% at \(Q\text{.}\) Therefore, \(T\left(\twovec{1000}{0}\right) = \twovec{700}{300}\text{.}\) Since \(T\left(\twovec{1000}{0}\right) = 1000T\left(\twovec{1}{0}\right)\text{,}\) we see that \(T\left(\twovec{1}{0}\right) = \twovec{0.7}{0.3}\text{.}\)
