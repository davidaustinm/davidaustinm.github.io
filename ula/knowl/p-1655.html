Since \(B\neq0\text{,}\) there is a nonzero vector \(\xvec\) such that \(B\xvec\neq\zerovec\text{.}\) However, we must have \(AB\xvec=\zerovec\text{,}\) which means that \(A(B\xvec) = \zerovec\text{.}\) There is therefore a nontrivial solution to the homogeneous equation \(A\yvec=\zerovec\) so the columns of \(A\) are linearly dependent.
