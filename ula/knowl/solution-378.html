Solution 7.2.2.1.

  1. \(q_D(\xvec) = 3x_1^2 - x_2^2\) and \(q_D\left(\twovec2{-4}\right) = -4\)

  2. \(q_A(\xvec) = 2x_1^2 + 10x_1x_2-3x_2^2\) and \(q_A\left(\twovec2{-1}\right) = -15\)

  3. \(\displaystyle A=\begin{bmatrix} 3 \amp -2 \\ -2 \amp 4 \\ \end{bmatrix}\)

  4. Notice that \(q(2\xvec) = (2\xvec)\cdot(A(2\xvec)) = 4\xvec\cdot(A\xvec) = 4q(\xvec)\text{.}\) In the same way, we have \(q(-\xvec) = q(\xvec)\) and \(q(10\xvec) = 100q(\xvec)\text{.}\)

  5. \(\displaystyle q_A(\xvec) = \xvec\cdot(A\xvec) = -4\xvec\cdot\xvec = -4(49) = -196\)
