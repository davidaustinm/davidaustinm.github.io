This activity shows us two ways to find the PageRank vector. In the first, we determine a steady-state vector directly by finding a description of the eigenspace \(E_1\) and then finding the appropriate scalar multiple of a basis vector that gives us the steady-state vector. To find a description of the eigenspace \(E_1\text{,}\) however, we need to find the null space \(\nul(G-I)\text{.}\) Remember that the real Internet has 30 trillion pages so finding \(\nul(G-I)\) requires us to row reduce a matrix with 30 trillion rows and columns.
