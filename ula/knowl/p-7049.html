As the examples in ActivityÂ 7.1.3 illustrate, the Spectral Theorem implies a number of things. Namely, if \(A\) is a symmetric \(m\times m\) matrix, then

  • the eigenvalues of \(A\) are real.

  • there is a basis of \(\real^m\) consisting of eigenvectors.
