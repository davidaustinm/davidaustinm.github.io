Once again, we can see this algebraically. Asking if the vector \(\bvec\) is in the span of \(\vvec\) and \(\wvec\) is the same as asking if the linear system

\begin{equation*} \begin{aligned} \left[\begin{array}{rr} \vvec \amp \wvec \end{array}\right] \xvec \amp {}={} \bvec \\ \\ \left[\begin{array}{rr} 2 \amp 1 \\ 1 \amp 2 \\ \end{array}\right] \xvec \amp {}={} \bvec \\ \end{aligned} \end{equation*}

is consistent.
