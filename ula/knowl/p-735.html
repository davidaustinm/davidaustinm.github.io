Every vector \(\bvec\) can be expressed as a linear combination of \(\vvec_1\text{,}\) \(\vvec_2\text{,}\) and \(\vvec_3\) in exactly one way because \(\left[\begin{array}{rrr} \vvec_1 \amp \vvec_2 \amp \vvec_3 \end{array}\right]\) has a pivot position in every row and every column.
