When \(p=0\text{,}\) we have a saddle. Notice that \(R\) will go extinct if the initial population of \(R\) is zero. As we increase \(p\text{,}\) we still have a saddle, but \(R\) will now go extinct when the initial \(S\) population is positive. Eventually, when \(p=1\text{,}\) we see that \(R\) will go extinct as long as \(R_0\gt S_0\text{.}\)
