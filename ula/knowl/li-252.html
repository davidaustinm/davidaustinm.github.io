Item 1.3.5.4.a.

Writing equations for the amount of charcoal and the amount of potassium nitrate, we have

\begin{equation*} \begin{alignedat}{3} 0.6x \amp {}+{} \amp 0.5y \amp {}={} \amp 100.3 \\ 0.3x \amp {}+{} \amp 0.2y \amp {}={} \amp 46.9\text{.} \\ \end{alignedat} \end{equation*}

Solving this gives \(x=113\) pounds and \(y=65\) pounds.
