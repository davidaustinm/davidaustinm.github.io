An anecdote

  • Disability Resources contacted the GVSU math department since a visually-impaired student would be enrolled in an algebra class in Winter 2023.

  • The textbook publisher wanted $40,000 for a braille edition of the textbook.

  • Rob Beezer, the founder of PreTeXt, converted Matt Boelkins' open precalculus textbook Active Prelude to braille.

  • I created tactile versions of (almost) all the diagrams.

  • Total cost: $0.
